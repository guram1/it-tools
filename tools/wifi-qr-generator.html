About WiFi QR Codes

WiFi QR codes allow users to connect to your wireless network instantly by scanning the code with their smartphone camera. No need to manually type long passwords or share credentials insecurely.

WiFi QR Code Format

The standard WiFi QR code format is: WIFI:T:WPA;S:NetworkName;P:Password;;

This format is supported by iOS (11+), Android, and most QR scanner apps.
